There's plenty to do in and around Manchester, VT and we hope you get a chance to explore the area. Just make sure you save your energy for the dance floor!

Hiking

For those who want to stretch their legs before the big event, there are many hiking and walking trails nearby. Two options are Equinox Mountain's 6-mile trail and Prospect Rock's 3-mile trail. Cell service can be spotty so be sure to bring a map!

Equinox Mountain Prospect Rock Trail

Shopping

Manchester has a friendly downtown with several boutiques, coffee shops, and restaurants. It's also home to the Orvis Flagship store and several designer outlets.

Manchester Designer Outlets Manchester Shops

Golf

There are a few golf courses open to the public around Manchester Village. If you're looking to stay close by, The Golf Club at Equinox is right down the street from the Kimpton Taconic.
