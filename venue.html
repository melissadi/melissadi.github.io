Kimpton Taconic

3835 Main Street

The Kimpton Taconic is a small and friendly hotel located in Manchester Village. We felt right at home at the Taconic and hope you do, too!

Getting There

The Taconic is a 3.5 hour drive from Boston Logan Airport, or 3 hours if you’re Melissa’s mom.

There is plenty of parking at the hotel, but please note that Manchester is out of the Uber/Lyft circuit. We will provide a shuttle service after the reception for guests who choose to leave a car at the Kimpton Taconic overnight.
